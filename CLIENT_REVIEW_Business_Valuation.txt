VALUATION PURPOSES (4 cards)
_____________________________________________________________________

CARD 1 - Litigation Support:
Business valuations for shareholder disputes, breach of contract cases, and other litigation matters requiring expert valuation testimony.

CARD 2 - Mergers & Acquisitions:
Valuation analysis for business sales, acquisitions, mergers, and strategic transactions to support negotiation and decision-making.

CARD 3 - Tax & Estate Planning:
Business valuations for gift and estate tax purposes, tax planning strategies, and compliance with IRS requirements for business transfers.

CARD 4 - Financial Reporting:
Valuations for financial reporting purposes, including purchase price allocations, impairment testing, and fair value measurements.

VALUATION APPROACHES (3 boxes)
_____________________________________________________________________

BOX 1 - Income Approach:

Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) analysis and capitalization of earnings methods that focus on the business's ability to generate future economic benefits.

• Discounted Cash Flow Analysis
• Capitalization of Earnings
• Risk Assessment and Discount Rate Analysis

BOX 2 - Market Approach:

Comparative analysis using market data from similar businesses, including public company multiples and transaction data.

• Guideline Public Company Method
• Guideline Transaction Method
• Market Multiple Analysis

BOX 3 - Asset Approach:

Analysis of the business's underlying assets and liabilities, including tangible and intangible asset valuations.

• Adjusted Net Asset Method
• Liquidation Value Analysis
• Intangible Asset Valuation
